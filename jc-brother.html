How the JC Written section is marked

Q17 — Blog (30 marks total)
Content (10 marks): 5 bullet points × 2 marks each. Each point: 2m if clear, relevant and developed. 1m if partially clear or not developed. 0m if unclear or irrelevant.

Expression (20 marks): One global mark for the quality of your Spanish across the whole answer. Top (17-20): rich vocabulary, correct tenses. Middle (10-16): very good vocabulary, mostly correct tenses. Lower (5-9): reasonable vocabulary, mostly incorrect tenses. Bottom (1-4): limited vocabulary and tenses.

⚠️ You must answer ALL 5 points to be eligible for the Top expression band. Miss one and your expression mark is capped.
Q18 — Response (40 marks total)
Content (16 marks): 8 points × 2 marks each. Same system — developed and relevant = 2m, partial = 1m, unclear = 0m.

Expression (24 marks): Same global system. Top (21-24): rich vocabulary, correct tenses. Middle (13-20): very good, mostly correct. Lower (7-12): reasonable, mostly incorrect. Bottom (1-6): limited.

⚠️ Must answer ALL 8 points for the Top expression band.
The golden rule
Never leave a point blank. A weak attempt at a point still gets 1 mark for content AND keeps your expression band options open. A blank gets 0 and caps your expression mark. Always write something for every point.
